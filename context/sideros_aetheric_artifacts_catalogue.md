# Sideros — The Aetheric Artifacts Catalogue

*Every magical and Aether-worked item in Sideros, gathered into one browsable reference: name,
rarity, attunement, cultural provenance, and effect. This consolidates the rarity ladder, the
Accord-Maker Infusions, the cultural anointments and materials, the Beast-Mantles, the Apex Fragment
reagents, the cultural accessories, the named relics, and the Sideros-specific materials — the
itemized list the world didn't have in one place.*

---

## How items work in Sideros

Magical items are called **Aetheric Artifacts**. They are sorted by **rarity** (which guides both
value and how often they turn up), by whether they require **attunement**, and by **cultural
provenance** — every Artifact carries the marks of the tradition that made it, and most cultures can
read an Artifact's origin on sight (Acuity Active Resonance, Threshold 12).

- **Attunement.** A character may attune to up to **three** Aetheric Artifacts at once. Attunement
  takes a Brief Attunement of focused interaction (the Artifact tells you what it needs). A fourth
  requires breaking one of the existing three. Apex-grade artifacts cannot be attuned — *they* attune
  to the holder, often without consent.
- **Anointed ≠ enchanted.** Cultural anointments and special materials are *prepared*, not
  *enchanted*. They bypass only the specific resistance they're designed for — a Cold-Iron Tipped
  blade still won't harm a creature resistant to nonmagical weapons unless that creature also has the
  Fae/Otherworld vulnerability the anointment addresses.
- **Provenance matters.** A Resonance-Seeker-forged +1 weapon reads differently from an
  Accord-Maker-Infused one (the latter isn't technically magical — it's *contractually enhanced*).

## II. Anointed Weapons — cultural smith upgrades

Below true magic weaponry is the deep tradition of **anointment**: a smith of a tradition *prepares*
a mundane weapon against a category of foe. The weapon doesn't become magical — it becomes prepared.
Work takes 3–7 days; **one anointment at a time**; permanent until the weapon is destroyed or
re-forged.

| Anointment | Tradition | Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Cold-Iron Tipped** | Tuathan Hollow-Smith | 75 Marks | Full damage to Fae-Blooded, Otherworld, and Fade-Bleed creatures regardless of their Aetheric defenses. |
| **Sun-Kissed Anointed** | Khem-Ma'at Solar Forge | 150 Marks | Counts as Sun-Kissed Adamas vs. Void-Husks/undead/Hollow Serpent emanations; +1d4 radiant; glows near Void corruption. |
| **Aether-Quenched** | Dunmarim Deep-Forge | 200 Marks | Functions normally inside the Stillness; +1 Strike Resonance within any Stillness zone. |
| **Moon-Etched** | Tuathan Illusion-Smith | 100 Marks | Concealed/phased/illusion-warded targets can't avoid the weapon; pattern visible only in moonlight. |
| **Bog-Etched** | Kurgan Bog-Keeper Smith | 125 Marks | On a hit, suppress one condition (Soul-Swayed, Dread-Struck, Corrupted, Resonance-Shattered) for 1 Pulse; 1×/encounter. |
| **Beast-Bound** | Beast-cultural smith (sign-specific) | 100 / 200 Marks | +1d6 damage to creatures of one chosen sign's lineage; can't be re-targeted. |
| **Lead-Suppressed** | Comet-Magi anti-Void inscription | 250 Marks | On a hit vs. a Lead-Scale carrier, target Wards (Threshold 14) or loses one Lead-Scale level; registers in Kallos-Sworn records. |
| **Star-Inked** | Stellar Cartographer (Sagittarian) | 175 Marks | 1×/Deep Attunement, Read a target's Fate Number; on success +1d6 on the next Strike against them. |

## III. Material Upgrades — weapon-defining materials

A weapon is made of **one** primary material; anointments may layer on top. Pricing is a multiplier
on the base weapon cost and requires the material's tradition-smith.

| Material | Cost | Property | Smith |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Black Iron** | ×5 | Works in the Stillness; +1 damage; unbreakable by nonmagical means | Dunmarim Deep-Forge |
| **Star-Metal** | ×8 | Counts as silvered; +1d4 radiant vs. undead/Void-Husks; never lost (findable within 24 hrs) | Sagittarian Star-Reading clan |
| **Sun-Kissed Adamas** (worked) | ×10 | Counts as magical; +1d6 vs. Void-Husks/undead/Hollow Serpent; sheds dim light; (worked metal: +2 Strike, auto-hit vs. Void-Husks/undead) | Khem-Ma'at Solar Forge |
| **Hearthwood Haft** | +200 Marks | Immune to fire; haft never shatters (shafted weapons only) | Mori-Ōkami Deep Earth Clan |
| **Stygian-Edged** | ×6 | On a hit, target Wards (Threshold 14) or a secret becomes briefly visible to you (blades only) | Bog-Keeper / Storm-Iyanifa smith |
| **Tuathan Bronze** | ×3 | Accepts inscriptions/anointments at 2× efficiency (anointments cost half); sacred to Fae-Blooded | Tuathan Hollow-Smith |
| **Obsidian-Edged** | ×2 | Adds Bleeding (1 dmg next Moment unless staunched); fragile (re-knap at +50 Marks) | K'uh-la'an volcanic workshop |

*Notable combos:* Black Iron + Aether-Quenched = +2 damage in the Stillness. Star-Metal +
Lead-Suppressed = registers in both Star-Reading and Kallos-Sworn records (politically loaded).
Stygian-Edged + Cold-Iron repel each other — neither works.

## IV. Beast-Mantles & Beast-domain artifacts

A **Beast-Mantle** is an artifact woven from a Legendary Beast's domain — one tradition per Beast,
in **lesser** (Rare) and **full** (Very Rare) grades. Named examples in canon include the
**Sky-Witness Cloak** and the **Sun-Sworn Mantle** (worn by Solar Chivalry Knight-Captains) and the
**Crystal-Stinger Vest** (Scorpio/Stygian Scorpion domain). Each grants powers aligned to its
Beast's nature (flight and far-sight, solar ward, venom and secret-sight, and so on).

The apex objects of a Beast's domain — the **lair-boons** harvested or gifted at a Beast's site —
are catalogued with the Points-of-Interest guide; the confirmed campaign items appear under Named &
Legendary Relics below (Celestial Honey, the Shed Roc Feather, the Crystal-Stinger, the Star-Metal
Antler).

## V. Apex Fragment Reagents — consumables

Not items but **pieces of a Beast itself**, consumed in a single Working of that Beast's domain.
Domain alignment is strict: a Sagittarius antler tine powers navigation/prophecy/star-reading and
nothing else.

| Tier | Source | Cost | Effect (consumed) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **T1 · Peripheral Trace** | Border-of-territory emanations | 10–50 Marks | Cast a domain-aligned Working with Resonant Fortune on its Threshold. |
| **T2 · Resonant Fragment** | Mid-territory emanations | 75–250 Marks | The Channel spent on a domain Working is restored — effectively a free cast. |
| **T3 · Resonant Core** | Deep-territory (with permission) | 400–800 Marks | Cast a domain Working one tier above your highest Channel; 1×/Deep Attunement. |
| **T4 · Apex Harvest** | The Beast's tacit permission | Not for sale | Cast any one domain Working free of a Channel, at the upper bound of all rolls. |
| **T5 · Resonant Binding Component** | The ritual fragments themselves | Plot-critical | Enables cosmic-tier rituals only; the Aether refuses lesser use. |
