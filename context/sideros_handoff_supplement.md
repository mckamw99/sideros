## LEAD-SCALE CORRUPTION — FULL MECHANICS

A 12-point gauge tracking Void-energy exposure. Zero = clean. Twelve = threshold of no return. Four stages of three points each.

### Stage 1 — Tempered (1–3)
- Aetheric Channels recover 1 fewer per rest
- Resonant Fortune on Zodiac Attunement Active Resonances becomes Resonant Discord
- DC for detecting character's zodiac resonance increases by 2
- *Visual:* Faint grey-silver discoloration at fingertips. Barely noticeable at 1–2; obvious to Weave-sensitive observers at 3.
- *Experience:* Zodiac connection feels muffled — present but like hearing through a wall.

### Stage 2 — Hardened (4–6)
- Maximum Aetheric Channels reduced by 2 tiers
- Warding Resonances against Void-energy effects at Resonant Discord
- Active Resonances involving Weaving or zodiac phenomena: Threshold +3
- Bond abilities function normally but Bond-mates feel the dampening
- *Visual:* Grey-silver to wrists and forearms. Metallic sheen in bright light.

### Stage 3 — Sealed (7–9)
- Cannot access Aetheric Channels 5th tier+ without Warding Resonance DC 16 (fail = Channel wasted)
- All Weaving Active Resonances at Resonant Discord
- Bond abilities operate one tier lower (Triad = Paired)
- *Visual:* Discoloration reaches shoulders, collarbone, face edges. Affected skin absorbs light in darkness.

### Stage 4 — Void-Touched (10–12)
- All Weaving requires Warding Resonance DC 18 or Void Surge (3d10 void damage to caster + 1 Lead-Scale to all within 10 ft)
- Bond connection almost imperceptible to character; Bond-mates can still feel them (increasingly desperate)
- Brief episodes of absent agency — more frequent approaching 12
- At 12: becomes a Void-Husk (character death / major narrative event)
- *Visual:* Eyes periodically flash void-dark (Te Kapo quality). Skin partially crystallized in Lead-Scale patterns.

### Acquisition Rate

### Reduction

## VOID-HUSK STAT BLOCKS

Void-Husks are what mortals become at Stage 4 Lead-Scale completion. Alive, metabolizing, but without individual will. Coordinated without communication. Prioritize Bond-mates and high-Aetheric individuals. Killing one does not disrupt others.

### Tier 1 — The Faded
*Fresh corruption. Retains physical capability. Less networked.*
- **Vitality:** 32 (5d8+10) · **Speed:** 30 ft · **Mastery Bonus:** +2
- FOR +2, SWI +1, END +2, ATT −2, ACU −1, RES −3
- **Void Coordination:** Shares Pulse Order with Husks within 60 ft regardless of individual rolls.
- **Aetheric Hunger:** Resonant Fortune vs. targets with active Aetheric Channels or Bond resonance.
- **Lead-Scale Touch:** +4 to hit · 1d6+2 bludgeoning + 1 Lead-Scale level (END DC 13 negates corruption).
- **Restoration:** 4+ Weep-Stone doses within 1 hour of Husking, or Kanya Siddha.
- *Visual:* Eyes drained to pale grey. Ashen skin. Unhurried, purposeless movement.

### Tier 2 — The Hollowed
*Identity gone. Fully networked. Physical tells visible.*
- **Vitality:** 65 (10d8+20) · **Speed:** 30 ft (min 15 ft) · **Mastery Bonus:** +3
- FOR +3, SWI +2, END +3, ATT −4, ACU −2, RES −4
- **Void Coordination (Extended):** 120 ft. When damaged, all nearby Husks know attacker's location.
- **Consuming Presence:** Creatures starting Moment within 10 ft gain 1 Lead-Scale level (END DC 14 negates).
- **Null Aura:** Weaving within 30 ft costs 1 extra Channel tier. No cantrips within 15 ft.
- **Void Resistance:** Resistant to all non-magical damage. Immune to void damage.
- **Lead-Scale Strike:** +5 to hit · 2d8+3 void damage + 2 Lead-Scale levels (END DC 15 negates corruption).
- **Unravel Bond (1/encounter):** One bonded creature within 60 ft. Bond abilities suppressed until end of next Moment (ATT DC 16 negates).
- **Restoration:** Kanya Siddha only.
- *Visual:* Eyes flat silver. Metallic veining in bright light. Eerie synchronized movement. Echo-quality voice.

### Tier 3 — The Consumed
*Deep corruption. Was a significant practitioner or Bond-member. Retains echo of former capabilities.*
- **Vitality:** 120 (16d10+32) · **Speed:** 35 ft · **Mastery Bonus:** +5
- FOR +4, SWI +3, END +4, ATT +1 (corrupted), ACU +0, RES −2
- **Void Coordination (Total):** Unlimited range within 1 mile. The Consumed knows everything every Husk in range knows.
- **Void Presence:** All creatures within 30 ft: Warding Resonances vs. Void-energy −3.
- **Lead-Scale Grasp:** +7 to hit · 3d10+4 void damage. END DC 16 or +2 Lead-Scale levels. Grapples on hit (Escape DC 17).
- **Corrupted Working (Recharge 5–6):** Echo of former ability, Void-infused. Concordance determines based on what they were. Deals void damage + 3 Lead-Scale levels on failed ATT DC 17.
- **Bond Severance Attempt (1/encounter):** One Triad+ bonded creature within 30 ft. Target + one Bond-mate: RES DC 18 or bond resonance severed for 1 hour. On natural 1: permanent catastrophic severance.
- **Restoration:** Cannot be restored.
- *Visual:* Void-black eyes (no iris/pupil). Skin partially crystallized. Slightly taller than pre-corruption. Does not walk — manifests at positions.

## THE ARROW — WHY THEY CAN BE IN RIVEN'S QUAD BOND

The conversion at the Night of Kallos broke the Fourth Architect's Loom thread — not ended normally (the way a natural death ends a thread) but *ruptured* by the catastrophic Bond severance. The conversion then created an entirely new Loom thread: the Arrow.

From the Loom's perspective, the Fourth Architect is a broken thread from four thousand years ago, and the Arrow is a separate, new entity. The Loom has no mechanism for tracking conversions across death. The Arrow's Quad Bond with Riven is genuine — they are not carrying the prior Bond membership because their original Loom thread is gone.

The Wound recognizes the Arrow's frequency. The Loom cannot categorize the connection. This is why the reveal requires the specific proximity of Aethar to the Arrow — the Loom won't announce it, only the Wound's resonance recognition will.

## THE ARROW'S LOOM THREAD — ADDITIONAL DETAIL

When the party meets Riven and the Arrow is eventually revealed as a Scion, the second and most devastating revelation is Layer 3 — that the Arrow is the Fourth Architect. Here is the specific information to hold:

**What the Arrow reported to the Kept:** Who killed the Fourth Architect. The Arrow did this without knowing they were reporting their own murder.

**What the Kept has held for 3,900 years:** The answer to who killed the Fourth Architect. The Kept's shift condition is when this secret finally produces the action it was preserved to enable. Once the party acts on the Night of Kallos truth, the Kept shifts.

**What happens when Aethar learns:** His memory was altered to prevent this knowledge from landing. The Wound might alter it again. Or four thousand years of the false version, finally confronted with the truth, might break through in a way the Wound cannot prevent. The Arrow being present — the Fourth Architect standing in front of him — is the specific condition under which the Wound cannot simply replace what Aethar hears. The frequency registers before the alteration mechanism can act.

**What Serath and Drava will experience:** They know who the Fourth Architect was. They believe the Fourth Architect died. When they learn the Arrow is still present — that they have been grieving someone who didn't die, that the inverted bond was formed around a grief that is not what they thought — the inverted bond's emotional foundation shifts. This may be the key to breaking it without requiring the Binding's completion first.
