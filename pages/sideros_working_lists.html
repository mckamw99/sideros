Sideros / Astrolan — Aetheric Working Reference

Working Lists

Which Workings are available to which Paths — the Comet-Mage Formulation Ledger, Zodiac Priest domain Workings, Star-Born innate list, and the full Path access table.

Not every Weave-capable Path draws from the same Working pool. The Comet-Mage learns from a structured Formulation Ledger; the Zodiac Priest accesses domain Workings tied to their sign; the Star-Born carries an innate list they cannot freely expand. This table shows which Working pools each Path draws from and how many Channels they have at each Depth.

The Channel Tier System: Workings are organized in tiers 1–9. A character's Channels are likewise tiered — a 1st-tier Channel powers 1st-tier Workings, a 3rd-tier Channel powers any Working up to 3rd tier, etc. You can always spend a higher-tier Channel on a lower-tier Working. Some Workings scale — they become more powerful when powered by a higher-tier Channel (indicated in the Working's description).

The Comet-Mage's Formulation Ledger is the most complete Working list in the world — the systematic product of centuries of Kallos-Sworn and Argent Spire research. Comet-Magi prepare Workings daily from their Ledger by selecting which Formulations to hold active. They can learn new Formulations by copying them from other Ledgers, scrolls, or Aetheric inscriptions found in the world.

Weaving Attribute: Acuity. Working Threshold: 8 + Mastery Bonus + Acuity modifier.

Tier 1 Formulations
Aetheric Missile
T1 · Aether · Instant
Three bolts of Aetheric force, each dealing 1d4+1 force damage, hitting automatically. Each additional Channel tier adds one bolt. Cannot miss. Does not require a Strike Resonance.
Detect Aetheric
T1 · Divination · S.R.
For up to 10 minutes (Sustained Resonance), sense the presence and location of Aetheric effects within 30 ft. Know the school but not the specific Working. Blocked by 1 ft of lead or a thin sheet of Aether-Dampening Ore.
Shroud Passage
T1 · Illusion · S.R.
Sustained Resonance, up to 1 hour. The target creature becomes invisible until they attack, Weave a Working, or the Working ends. Anything the target is wearing or carrying is also invisible.
Thread-Shield
T1 · Abjuration · Reflex
Reflex: when hit by a Strike Resonance or Aetheric Missile, gain +5 Guard Rating for that hit and immunity to Aetheric Missile specifically. Lasts until your next Moment.
Charm Weave
T1 · Compulsion · 1 hour
One humanoid within 30 ft makes a Resonance Warding Resonance or becomes Soul-Swayed by you for 1 hour. Ends if you or allies harm the target.
Burning Hands
T1 · Evocation · Instant
15-ft cone. Each creature makes Swiftness Warding Resonance DC = Working Threshold or takes 3d6 fire (half on success). Each additional Channel tier: +1d6.
Illusory Figure
T1 · Illusion · S.R. up to 10 min
Create a visual illusion of an object, creature, or phenomenon within 30 ft. No larger than a 10-ft cube. Static — cannot produce sound, smell, or movement. Acuity Active Resonance DC = Working Threshold to disbelieve on inspection.
Sleep Weave
T1 · Enchantment · Instant
Roll 5d8. Creatures within 20 ft are affected in ascending order of Vitality until the total is consumed — each falls unconscious (Loom-Suspended) for 1 minute. Undead and immune creatures are skipped. Each additional Channel tier: +2d8.
Mage Armour
T1 · Abjuration · 8 hours
Touch a willing creature not wearing armor: Guard Rating becomes 13 + Swiftness modifier for the duration. Dismissed if they don armor.
Identify
T1 · Divination · 1 minute ritual
Touch an Aetheric Artifact or Working-imbued object: learn its properties, how to use it, and whether it is cursed. Also: touch a creature to learn all Workings currently affecting them.
Tier 2 Formulations
Web
T2 · Conjuration · S.R. up to 1 hour
Fill a 20-ft cube with thick Aetheric webbing. The area is difficult terrain. Each creature starting their Moment in the area makes Swiftness Warding Resonance DC = Working Threshold or is Seized. Can be burned away (5 fire damage clears a 5-ft cube).
Aetheric Locks
T2 · Abjuration · Until dispelled
Touch a door, window, or portal: it becomes Aetherically sealed. Forcing it open requires Forcefulness Active Resonance DC = Working Threshold + 10. You and designated creatures pass freely. Knock Working suppresses it for 10 minutes.
Shatter
T2 · Evocation · Instant
Painful ringing sound erupts from a point within 60 ft. Each creature in a 10-ft sphere makes Endurance Warding Resonance DC = Working Threshold or takes 3d8 thunder (half on success). Nonmagical objects automatically take damage and may shatter. Each additional Channel tier: +1d8.
Invisibility
T2 · Illusion · S.R. up to 1 hour
A creature becomes invisible for the duration. Ends when they attack or Weave. At 3rd tier: Greater Invisibility — does not end when attacking.
Misty Step
T2 · Conjuration · Instinct
Wreathed in silver mist, teleport up to 30 ft to an unoccupied space you can see. Does not provoke Reflex Strikes.
Hold Person
T2 · Enchantment · S.R. up to 1 min
One humanoid within 60 ft makes Resonance Warding Resonance DC = Working Threshold or becomes Loom-Suspended. May repeat the Warding Resonance at the end of each of their Moments. Each additional Channel tier: +1 additional target.
Scorching Ray
T2 · Evocation · Instant
Create three rays of fire. Strike Resonance for each (Acuity-based). Hit: 2d6 fire. Each additional Channel tier: +1 ray.
See Invisibility
T2 · Divination · 1 hour
You can see invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible, and see into the Ethereal Plane up to 60 ft.
Tier 3 Formulations
Fireball
T3 · Evocation · Instant
A bright streak from your fingertip explodes at a point within 150 ft into a 20-ft radius sphere of fire. Each creature makes Swiftness Warding Resonance DC = Working Threshold or takes 8d6 fire (half on success). Each additional Channel tier: +1d6.
Counterspell
T3 · Abjuration · Reflex
Reflex: when a creature within 60 ft Weaves a Working, attempt to disrupt it. If the Working is T3 or lower, it automatically fails. If higher, make Acuity Active Resonance DC = 10 + Working's tier. On success, it fails.
Fly
T3 · Transmutation · S.R. up to 10 min
Touch a willing creature: they gain fly speed of 60 ft for the duration. If the Working ends while aloft, they fall.
Haste
T3 · Transmutation · S.R. up to 1 min
One willing creature within 30 ft: doubled speed; +2 Guard Rating; Resonant Fortune on Swiftness Warding Resonances; gains one additional Act per Moment (only for Strike, Dash, Disengage, or Use Object). When Haste ends, they cannot move or act until after their next Moment (lethargy).
Lightning Bolt
T3 · Evocation · Instant
100-ft line, 5 ft wide. Each creature makes Swiftness Warding Resonance DC = Working Threshold or takes 8d6 lightning (half on success). Each additional Channel tier: +1d6.
Dispel Aetheric
T3 · Abjuration · Instant
Choose one creature, object, or Aetheric effect within 120 ft. Any Working T3 or lower on the target automatically ends. For higher-tier Workings, make Acuity Active Resonance DC = 10 + the Working's tier. At 4th tier: automatically dispel any Working T4 or lower; no roll required.
Tier 4–5 Formulations
Dimension Door
T4 · Conjuration · Instant
Teleport yourself and one willing creature of your choice within 5 ft to any location within 500 ft that you can see, visualize, or describe by direction and distance. Both arrive in the nearest unoccupied spaces.
Greater Invisibility
T4 · Illusion · S.R. up to 1 min
Target creature becomes invisible for the duration. Unlike standard Invisibility, this does not end when the target attacks or Weaves a Working.
Arcane Eye
T4 · Divination · S.R. up to 1 hour
Create an invisible Aetheric eye within 30 ft that you can see through. Darkvision 30 ft. Move up to 30 ft per Act (tight spaces: Acuity Active Resonance DC 15). Cannot pass through solid barriers.
Teleportation Circle
T5 · Conjuration · 1 minute ritual
Draw a 10-ft diameter circle and speak a sequence of sigils. A portal opens to a permanent teleportation circle whose sigil sequence you know. All creatures stepping through arrive at the destination. The circle fades after 1 Pulse.
Cone of Cold
T5 · Evocation · Instant
60-ft cone: each creature makes Endurance Warding Resonance DC = Working Threshold or takes 8d8 cold (half on success). Creatures reduced to 0 Vitality by this Working freeze solid.
Wall of Force
T5 · Evocation · S.R. up to 10 min
An invisible wall of Aetheric force springs into existence within 120 ft. Up to ten panels (each 10 ft × 10 ft), arranged into any shape. Immune to all damage. Dispel Aetheric (T5) can destroy it. Prevents passage of all matter and energy.
Higher Tier Formulations (6th–9th): Workings of the 6th tier and above are covered in the Argent Spire restricted records — the Concordance provides access to these when narratively appropriate. Notable high-tier Formulations include: True Sight (T6), Delayed Blast Fireball (T7), Power Word Stun (T8), Wish (T9 — theoretical only; no confirmed casting since Kallos era). The Zodiac Priest's Epic Tier extends to 10th–12th tier Workings; these are described in the Zodiac Priest Callings document.

The Zodiac Priest's power is domain-driven — they access a specific cluster of Workings tied to their zodiac sign's cosmic domain, always prepared regardless of daily Channel allocation. These domain Workings are in addition to their Universal Working list. A Zodiac Priest of Simha (Leo) always has the Leo domain Workings prepared; they do not need to spend preparation slots on them.

Weaving Attribute: Attunement. Working Threshold: 8 + Mastery Bonus + Attunement modifier.

The Star-Born's Working list is not learned — it is inherited. These Workings are expressions of Celestial ancestry rather than trained formulations. The Star-Born cannot freely swap Workings between Depths (unlike the Comet-Mage); they receive specific Workings at specific Depths, and those Workings are permanent. They cannot copy new Workings from Ledgers.

Weaving Attribute: Resonance. Working Threshold: 8 + Mastery Bonus + Resonance modifier. Channel recovery: Regain 2 Channels per tier on Brief Attunement for tiers 1–5.

Universal Workings are accessible to any Weave-capable Path, across all traditions. They represent the foundational layer of Aetheric expression that predates any specific school or tradition — the basic grammar of Working that emerged in the Precursor civilization and was never fully lost.

Universal Ambient Workings (No Channel Cost)
Light
Ambient · Touch
An object you touch sheds bright light in a 20-ft radius, dim light for another 20 ft, for 1 hour. The light's color is your choice. Extinguishing it ends the Working early.
Mending
Ambient · Touch · 1 minute
Repair a single break or tear in an object (up to 1 ft in any dimension). Returns the object to perfect condition but cannot restore Aetheric properties to damaged Artifacts.
Prestidigitation
Ambient · 30 ft · Instant
Create a minor sensory effect (sound, light, smell); light or extinguish a candle or torch; clean or dirty a cubic foot of material; chill, warm, or flavor one pound of nonliving material; make a small mark or symbol appear for 1 hour. At will.
Guidance
Ambient · Touch · S.R. up to 1 min
A willing creature you touch can roll 1d4 and add the result to one Active Resonance of their choice before the Working ends. Once used, the Working ends.
Universal Tier 1 Workings
Cure Wounds
T1 · Touch · Instant
A creature you touch regains 1d8 + Weaving Attribute modifier Vitality. Each additional Channel tier: +1d8.
Sanctuary
T1 · 30 ft · 1 min
Ward one creature. Any creature that targets the warded creature with a Strike Resonance or harmful Working must first make a Resonance Warding Resonance DC = Working Threshold; on fail, must choose a new target. Ends if the warded creature attacks or Weaves a harmful Working.
Command
T1 · 60 ft · Instant
One creature that can hear you makes a Resonance Warding Resonance DC = Working Threshold or follows one-word command on their next Moment (Approach, Drop, Flee, Grovel, Halt). Cannot command self-destructive actions. Each additional Channel tier: +1 target.
Detect Thoughts
T2 · 30 ft · S.R.
Probe a creature's surface thoughts (what they're actively thinking). Make an Attunement Active Resonance contested by their Resonance Active Resonance to probe deeper (memories, fears, secrets). Target becomes aware of the probing on a successful contest.
On Working Names: Sideros does not use D&D Working names in-world. The names here are labels for the Concordance's reference — in play, characters describe what they are doing in Sideros terms. A Comet-Mage doesn't cast Fireball; they Weave the Mesha Formulation of Combustive Release. A Zodiac Priest doesn't cast Cure Wounds; they Channel the Kanya Mantra of Restoration. The mechanical effects are identical; the fictional texture is different. The Concordance is encouraged to rename Workings per the tradition that Weaves them.
