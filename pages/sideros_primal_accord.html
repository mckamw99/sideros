The Primal Accord

Accord-Walkers · Arcadia Major

They do not believe the gods fell. They believe the gods chose a different form — and they learned to speak that language.

★ Original Path Vitality Die: d8 Alpha / Beta split by Calling Dual Resource

The Accord-Walker does not call themselves a druid, a shaman, or a nature priest. They call themselves Accord-Walkers — those who walk in accord with what the Beasts already are. The distinction matters. A priest mediates between their congregation and a distant power. An Accord-Walker has no congregation and recognizes no distance. The Legendary Beasts of Arcadia Major — the Nemean Lion, the Queen Bee of the Golden Hive, the Star-Metal Stag — are not objects of worship. They are colleagues, operating at a scale that requires translation rather than reverence.

The Arcadian theology that produces Accord-Walkers begins with a single premise: the Pantheonic Fall was a choice, not a catastrophe. The twelve gods did not lose their divinity. They redistributed it — from concentrated divine forms into distributed biological presence. Aries is not merely present in the Ashen Ram; Aries is the Ashen Ram, fully and completely. The form is different. The force is unchanged. Every Accord-Walker learns this theology before they learn their first Aspect — because understanding what the Beasts are determines how you relate to them, and how you relate to them determines what they'll share with you.

The Queen Bee's Golden Hive is the center of Accord-Walker training. The Bee-Priestess lineage has maintained unbroken contact with the Queen Bee for longer than any other institution has maintained contact with any Legendary Beast — four thousand years of continuous relationship, from before the Fall through the Fall itself and into the present era. The Bee-Priestess does not speak for the Queen Bee. She speaks with her, in a dialogue that has produced the most complete understanding of Legendary Beast cognition anywhere in the world. Every Accord-Walker who trains at the Golden Hive learns, eventually, that the Queen Bee remembers the gods before they became Beasts — and that her hive-mind carries the memory of what each zodiac force felt like before it wore its current form. This knowledge is the most carefully guarded secret in Arcadia Major. It is also the most dangerous thing an Accord-Walker can learn.

What distinguishes this Path
Two entirely separate resource systems that enable two entirely separate modes of power. Beast Resonance powers physical transformation and combat. Hive Attunement powers communal and information abilities. A player has to manage both simultaneously — and choose moment to moment which mode the situation demands.
What this Path is not
Not a wild shape replacement — Aspect Manifestation works differently. Not a nature-cleric — the Accord-Walker has no patron and performs no Benedictions. Not a summoner — the Beasts are not called, they are expressed. Not a pacifist — the Nemean Lion is not peaceful, and neither are its Accord-Walkers.
Primal Essence split
Alpha Accord-Walkers gravitate toward the Nemean Calling — expressing Beast power outward, leading through force of nature. Beta Accord-Walkers gravitate toward the Keeper and Hive Callings — sustaining ecosystems, maintaining communal awareness, holding the pattern together from within.
Relationship to Wolf-Shifters
Wolf-Shifters and Accord-Walkers are the two Paths most likely to encounter each other's transformation abilities and compare notes. The Omote-Ura Gauge is biological — it's what you are. Beast Resonance is attunement — it's what you're reaching toward. The Shōgun-Kami bond and the Beast Resonance system recognize each other as kin without being the same thing.
Beast Resonance
Transformation Resource
Accumulated attunement with specific Legendary Beasts — stored as a pool that powers Aspect Manifestation. Not a general resource: each Beast contributes its own Resonance Points to a separate sub-pool. You cannot spend Nemean Lion resonance to manifest a Stag Aspect. The Beasts are distinct.

Hive Attunement
Communal Resource
Connection to the Queen Bee's distributed consciousness — a shared awareness that extends beyond the individual. Fills through communal acts and shared experience; depletes when used for information, protection, and coordination abilities. Unique among Path resources: it can be shared with allies.
Beast Resonance — How It Works

Every Legendary Beast the Accord-Walker has directly encountered contributes to a separate Resonance sub-pool. A new encounter with a Beast increases that Beast's sub-pool maximum. Resonance Points within each sub-pool are spent to manifest that Beast's Aspect and refill during Brief or Deep Attunement.

Hive Attunement — How It Works

Hive Attunement measures the Accord-Walker's current depth of connection to the Queen Bee's distributed consciousness — the living network of shared awareness that the Golden Hive maintains across its 40-mile territory, and that an initiated Accord-Walker remains tethered to regardless of distance. It is not the Accord-Walker's personal resource. It belongs to the Hive. They are borrowing access to it.

6 Attunement: Full Communion — enter the Queen Bee's consciousness for 1 Pulse; receive her complete situational awareness for that Pulse (know the position, Vitality, and intent of every creature within 300 ft).
The Key Interaction
Beast Resonance and Hive Attunement do not share a pool and do not interfere with each other. An Accord-Walker can manifest a Full Aspect while simultaneously spending Hive Attunement on a Communal Whisper in the same encounter. The constraint is action economy, not resource economy — manifesting an Aspect costs an Act; spending Hive Attunement costs an Instinct for minor effects or an Act for major ones. Managing both systems well means reading the situation and knowing which mode it demands.

Aspect Manifestation is the Accord-Walker's answer to wild shape — and the two mechanics work differently enough that the comparison obscures more than it reveals. A wild shape user becomes the animal. An Accord-Walker expresses the Beast's cosmic frequency through their own body. They do not cease to be themselves. They become more themselves, filtered through a frequency that is larger than any individual form. The transformation is visible and unmistakable. It is not disguise. It is declaration.

Different Aspect tiers produce different degrees of transformation. A Minor Aspect is a single physical trait — slightly unnerving, easy to miss in poor lighting. A Full Aspect is impossible to ignore — the Accord-Walker's body partially takes on the Beast's morphology while retaining their own cognitive and social capacities. A Legendary Aspect approaches something the world has not seen since before the Fall: a mortal being briefly carrying a fragment of divine force at full expression.

Aspect Restrictions
One Aspect at a time — manifesting a new Aspect ends the previous one immediately.
Cognitive continuity — the Accord-Walker retains their own mind, memory, and personality at all Aspect tiers. They can speak, use their Disciplines, and make decisions normally. The Beast's instincts are present as a strong inclination, not a compulsion — except during Legendary Aspects, where the Beast's frequency is strong enough to produce behavioral pressure (the Concordance may describe this as an intrusive impulse that the Accord-Walker can resist but must acknowledge).
Equipment — light equipment (clothing, small items) merges with the Aspect harmlessly. Heavy armor, shields, and large weapons fall away during Full and Legendary Aspects. They reappear when the Aspect ends.
The Twin Dream-Fish exception — as noted in the table, the Dream-Fish cannot be expressed as an Aspect. An Accord-Walker who has encountered the Dream-Fish has a different relationship with them: once per Deep Attunement, they may spend 3 Hive Attunement to consult the Dream-Fish as a prophetic contact rather than manifesting their Aspect.
Beast Resonance & Initial Accord
Depth 1
The Beast Resonance system becomes active. Choose one Legendary Beast you have encountered — this is your Primary Beast, your first and deepest accord. You begin with a full pool (5 Resonance Points) for this Beast, and an empty pool for all others. The Beast Resonance system scales with Resonance Depth: pool maximum per Beast increases by 1 at Depths 4, 8, 12, and 16 (maximum 9 at Depth 16).

You also gain the Nature Tongue: you can communicate basic intent (not complex language) with any creature whose Beast sign you have encountered. A lion won't carry on a conversation with you, but it will receive a clear non-aggression signal and a specific request for safe passage. More Beasts encountered means more creature-families you can communicate with.
Hive Initiation
Depth 1
Through the Bee-Priestess's initiation ritual (completed before or during the campaign's opening), the Accord-Walker gains access to the Hive Attunement system. Begin each session with Hive Attunement equal to half your Attunement modifier (minimum 1, maximum 3). Full restore on Deep Attunement within the Hive's territory; restore half on Deep Attunement elsewhere.

The Hive Sense passive: even with 0 Hive Attunement, you always know the number of living creatures within 60 ft — not their position or identity, but their count. The Hive never fully disconnects.
Accord-Walker's Resilience
Depth 2
The Accord-Walker's body, repeatedly reconfigured through Aspect Manifestation, develops exceptional physical adaptability. They gain Mastery in all Endurance Warding Resonances. Additionally, they no longer need to make Attunement or Endurance checks to maintain an Aspect when they take damage — the Beast's frequency is stable enough to hold through physical disruption. This replaces the need for Sustained Resonance in Aspect Manifestation; Aspects do not require Sustained Resonance.
Second Beast Accord
Depth 3
The Accord-Walker deepens their practice sufficiently to hold attunement with a second Beast simultaneously. They may now have two Beast Resonance pools active at once — one Primary Beast and one Secondary Beast. The Secondary pool begins at the depth of the most recent encounter with that Beast (minimum 1 if encountered at all; 0 if not yet encountered).

This is also when the Calling is chosen — the specific expression of the Accord-Walker's practice. The Calling determines which Beast is most accessible and what additional abilities emerge from deeper accord.
Terrain Resonance
Depth 4
Choose two terrain types (forest, swamp, grassland, mountain, coastal, underground, arctic, desert). In these terrains: movement not impeded by natural difficult terrain; cannot be tracked by non-magical means; Resonant Fortune on Perception and Survival Active Resonances; Beast Resonance restores +1 per pool during Brief Attunement (in addition to normal restoration). Additional terrain types unlocked at Depths 8 and 12.

Terrain Resonance and the grid: An Accord-Walker whose chosen terrain includes the area around an active Ceque Line node feels the node's status immediately upon entering its range. They don't understand what they're perceiving — they feel it as a wrongness or rightness in the terrain, not as Aetheric data. A Ceque-Keeper working with an Accord-Walker in this way gains crucial early warning.
Hive Memory
Depth 6
The Accord-Walker's sustained connection to the Hive has granted them access to its deepest archive — the Queen Bee's memory of what the gods were before they became Beasts. Once per Deep Attunement, by spending 4 Hive Attunement and entering a 10-minute meditation, the Accord-Walker may ask the Concordance one question about what any zodiac force was like in Era I — its original divine nature, its relationship to the mortal world, what it protected, what it feared.

The answers are always accurate and always incomplete. The Hive remembered through sensation, not language. The information comes as impressions, emotional weight, and physical memory — not clear statements. An Accord-Walker who receives a Hive Memory and has an Eldren ally may find the two sources (Hive Memory + Ancestral Resonance) together produce something neither could generate alone.
Beast Chorus
Depth 10
The Accord-Walker can now manifest Minor Aspects from two different Beasts simultaneously — wearing one Beast's trait alongside another's. The combinations are limited only by physical compatibility (you cannot have the Nemean Lion's wings and the Crab's full carapace at the same time, but you can have the Lion's golden hide and the Stag's star-sense). Each Minor Aspect costs its normal 1 Resonance Point from the relevant Beast's pool.

Additionally: the Accord-Walker may spend 2 Hive Attunement to share an active Minor or Partial Aspect with one willing ally for 1 Pulse — the ally gains the Aspect's physical benefit for that Pulse only. This is the Hive extending what one member has to all members. The ally does not spend Resonance Points; the Accord-Walker's pool sustains the extension.
Full Accord
Depth 14
The Accord-Walker has reached sufficient depth of attunement that their Legendary Beast encounters begin to take on a different character. Their Primary Beast now recognizes them as a peer — as a creature expressing its frequency rather than borrowing it. This has practical effects:

• The Primary Beast will not attack them under any circumstances (though it may still act aggressively toward their allies).
• The Primary Beast may occasionally seek the Accord-Walker out — the Concordance may introduce Beast-initiated encounters where the Beast has information, a request, or simply the desire for contact with the one mortal who expresses its nature accurately.
• Legendary Aspect cost for the Primary Beast reduces to 3 Resonance Points.
• The Accord-Walker may attempt to request a meeting with their Primary Beast once per session without requiring a specific location — the Beast will come, or send a signal indicating when and where it is willing to be found.
The Living Accord
Depth 20
The Accord-Walker's attunement is now so deep that the distinction between their own Aetheric frequency and their Primary Beast's frequency has become genuinely blurred — not erased, but permeable. In practical terms: the Accord-Walker's body shifts subtly toward their Primary Beast's morphology at all times, regardless of active Aspect (the Nemean Lion Accord-Walker has gold in their irises permanently; the Stag Accord-Walker has antler-like bone ridges forming at their temples). These are not threatening — they are recognition marks.

Mechanically: The Minor Aspect of the Primary Beast is always active without cost. All Aspect costs for the Primary Beast are reduced by 1 (minimum 1). Once per Deep Attunement, the Accord-Walker may enter a Transcendent Accord — a state lasting up to 10 minutes in which they fully embody their Primary Beast's cosmic frequency without limit. During Transcendent Accord, the Legendary Aspect's effects persist for the full 10 minutes rather than 1 minute, and the Accord-Walker is recognized by all creatures as a genuine expression of that zodiac force. The Queen Bee refers to Accord-Walkers at this depth as "the ones who remembered what we are."

The three Callings of the Primal Accord represent three different answers to the question of what an accord with the Beasts is for. The Nemean Caller believes it is for confronting what threatens the natural order — directly, visibly, without apology. The Keeper believes it is for maintaining what exists — the slow, patient, invisible work of holding ecosystems together. The Bee-Speaker believes it is for communication — the translation between the world's forces that prevents catastrophic misunderstanding.

The Nemean Calling
Alpha-aspected · Combat

The Nemean Callers orient toward the Lion's aspect — direct force, visible presence, the willingness to be the thing that stands between a threat and what it's threatening. They are the Accord-Walkers most likely to be found in combat, and the most likely to be Alpha-aspected. Available at Depth 3.

Pride's Fury (Depth 3): When you manifest a Full or Legendary Aspect and reduce a creature to 0 Vitality in the same Pulse, you restore 1 Resonance Point to the manifested Beast's pool — the kill feeds the accord. Additionally, all allies who witnessed the reduction gain Resonant Fortune on their next Strike Resonance this encounter. The Lion's victory is the pride's victory.
Apex Predator (Depth 7): When in any Full or Legendary Aspect, you cannot be surprised. You always act in the first Pulse of any encounter, regardless of Pulse Order roll. Additionally, creatures below half their maximum Vitality have Resonant Discord on all Strike Resonances against you — the wounded do not challenge the apex without paying for it.
Legendary Resonance (Depth 11): Your Legendary Aspect for any Beast you have encountered at least three times costs 4 Resonance Points instead of 5. Additionally, during a Legendary Aspect, you may spend 2 Hive Attunement to broadcast the Aspect's presence to all creatures within 300 ft — every creature within range becomes instantly aware that something of Legendary Beast frequency is expressing nearby. Most creatures will reconsider their current course of action.
The Keeper's Calling
Beta-aspected · Stewardship

The Keepers hold ecosystems together — maintaining the patterns that allow life to continue, repairing damage to natural and Aetheric systems, and serving as the Accord-Walker tradition's connection to the Ceque Line system and the broader grid. They are often Beta-aspected and rarely seek recognition. Available at Depth 3.

Ecosystem Reading (Depth 3): In any natural environment, you automatically know: the health of the local ecosystem (thriving, stable, stressed, or dying); the presence and approximate location of any Aetheric disruption (grid node reactivation, Void-energy contamination, Ashen Coil blight-rot spread) within 1 mile; and which creature in the local ecosystem is in the greatest danger. This is passive, always active, and requires no roll. You are the ecosystem's awareness of itself.
Restoration Aspect (Depth 7): Once per Deep Attunement, when you maintain a Partial or Full Aspect for its full duration without ending it early, the Beast's Resonance pool refills by 2 at the Aspect's end rather than depleting further. The sustained expression deepens the accord rather than drawing it down. Additionally: when you use an Aspect for a purpose that protects or restores the natural world rather than combat, the Hive Attunement cost of any simultaneous Hive ability is reduced by 1.
Grid Warden (Depth 11): Your Terrain Resonance now extends to the Aetheric grid — you can feel Ceque Line nodes within 1 mile as distinct physical sensations, and you know immediately when a node changes state (dormant to active, active to Ophiuchus-reactivating). When you are present at a grid node activation (a Siddha speaking the correct mantra), you may spend 3 Hive Attunement to double the node's effect radius. You have become the living intersection of natural and Aetheric preservation.
The Bee-Speaker's Calling
Omega-aspected · Communion

The Bee-Speakers develop the deepest connection to the Hive Attunement system of any Accord-Walker tradition. They are the order's communicators, diplomats, and intelligence-gatherers — the ones who know what every creature in the area is feeling and can act on that knowledge faster than anyone else processes it. Available at Depth 3.

Hive Mastery (Depth 3): Your Hive Attunement maximum increases to 12. The cost of all Hive Attunement abilities is reduced by 1 (minimum 1). Additionally: once per Brief Attunement, you may spend 1 Hive Attunement to instantly know one specific piece of information about any creature you can currently sense through Hive Sense (their Vitality level, their current intent, their Fate Number, or their emotional state — choose one per use).
Communal Memory (Depth 7): The Hive's distributed memory is now accessible to you as a research tool. Once per session, by spending 4 Hive Attunement and 1 hour in communion, you may ask the Concordance one question about any event that occurred within the Queen Bee's 40-mile territory at any point in the last 500 years. The Hive was there. The Hive saw it. The Hive's answer comes in sensory impressions, but it comes accurately.

Additionally: you may now share Hive Attunement with a willing ally — transfer up to 3 points of your current pool to them; they may spend it as you would for 24 hours. The Hive recognizes them as an extension of you.
The Queen's Voice (Depth 11): Once per Deep Attunement, you may speak with the Queen Bee's full authority for 1 minute — your words carry the weight of the Hive's 4,000-year accumulated consciousness. During this time: any creature that hears you speak may not attack you or any creature you designate as protected (DC 18 Resonance Warding Resonance to resist — this is the Hive's collective will, not a Working); you may end any ongoing conflict between creatures within 60 ft by declaring the conflict finished (same Warding Resonance to resist). This is not compulsion — it is the weight of something very old and very certain expressing through you. Most creatures comply. Void-Husks and creatures with dissolved Fate Numbers are immune.

The Accord-Walker in the World

The Accord-Walker tradition is exclusively Arcadian in origin — it emerged from the Golden Hive and has never been successfully transplanted to another culture's framework. Other civilizations have tried. The Comet-Magi spent a century attempting to formalize Beast Resonance as an Aetheric sub-discipline. Their results were consistent: the Beasts refused to cooperate with practitioners who approached them as research subjects rather than peers. The Kallos-Sworn have three documented cases of would-be Accord-Walkers initiating without the Bee-Priestess's guidance — all three produced unstable Aspects that the practitioners could not control. The Hive did not intervene. The tradition is not exclusive, but it is specific: it requires the relationship before it provides the power.

Relationship with the Kallos-Sworn
Complicated. The Kallos-Sworn respect Accord-Walkers but do not fully understand them — the order's relationship with Legendary Beasts is one of cautious reverence and strategic resource-extraction (the Resonant Binding components). Accord-Walkers experience this as missing the point. An Accord-Walker who encounters Arch-Oracle Lyra will find someone who has thought more carefully about the Beasts than most Kallos-Sworn — and who has reached the limit of what thought without relationship can produce.
Relationship with the Ashen Coil
The Coil's dissolution agenda is comprehensible to an Accord-Walker in a way it may not be to other Paths — they understand entropy as a natural force, and some of what the Coil believes about the cosmos's direction is not entirely wrong. What the Coil gets wrong, from an Accord-Walker's perspective, is the assumption that dissolution requires assistance. The natural world dissolves and regenerates without help. Aethar is not accelerating a natural process — he is hijacking it.
The Bee-Priestess and the order's future
The current Bee-Priestess is aware that the Quint Bond prophecy is relevant to the Accord-Walker tradition — specifically that the Queen Bee has offered Celestial Honey, one of the Resonant Binding's five components, to exactly one person in the last 500 years. She has not told the Kallos-Sworn who that person was. She is waiting for the Accord-Walker who will ask the right question.
Grid-Point Seven and the Keeper Calling
Keeper Accord-Walkers are the most likely characters to detect Grid-Point Seven's reactivation before anyone else does — their Ecosystem Reading passive specifically identifies Aetheric disruptions within 1 mile. A Keeper who passes through Elethorn will feel something wrong about the grid before any Ceque-Keeper has formally identified the source. This is the Path's most direct campaign hook into the hidden Stage Four threat.
Starting Equipment
Primal Accord Starting Package
Package A (wilderness-oriented): A natural weapon of choice (staff of living wood, bone-handled blade, stone-weighted club), leather armor or hide armor, an explorer's pack, a Beast-sign token (a physical representation of your Primary Beast — claw, feather, carapace fragment — never taken by force, always given or found), and a letter of introduction from the Bee-Priestess to any Arcadian settlement.

Package B (hive-oriented): A quarterstaff, a healer's kit (10 uses), a diplomat's pack, a vial of Celestial Honey (narrative item — not the Resonant Binding component; a small personal gift from the Hive, providing one use of the Queen Bee's Domain ability as per the Ashé-Touched Kindred table), and the Bee-Priestess's mark (a tattoo or brand that identifies you to any being familiar with the Arcadian tradition).

Always: Record of at least one Legendary Beast encounter in your personal journal — the details of your first accord, in your own words. The Concordance should ask you to describe this encounter at the table before the first session begins. It matters.
The Question the Bee-Priestess Asks Every Candidate

"If the gods are not fallen — if they chose this form — what does your accord with them make you responsible for?"

There is no correct answer. The question is asked to see whether the candidate understands that there will always be one.
