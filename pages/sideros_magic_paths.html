The Five Paths to Magic

Magic in Sideros is a resonance with cosmic forces — not a simple birthright. Every tradition is a different answer to the same question: how does a mortal touch the Aether?

🔱
The Path of the Scattered Temples
Siddha-Magic — The most ancient and pure form of magic in Sideros

The Twelve Scattered Temples are the oldest magical institutions in the world — each a degraded remnant of Precursor civilization science, now practiced as spiritual discipline by the Eldren and their students. What the Siddha call mantras, the Precursors of Kallos called equations. The students do not know this.

How it is gained
Rigorous meditation, discipline, and study of Mantras (sound formulas) and Mudras (hand gestures). Years of practice under an Eldren Siddha master.
Source of power
Resonance with a specific Rashi (Zodiac sign). The temple's ley line to that sign's cosmic domain. Internal cultivation of Tapas (ascetic heat).
Associated class
Rāśi-Siddha (Monk) using Tapas Points. Also Zodiac Priests and Comet-Mages with temple training.
Hazard
Magic beyond Tapas capacity causes environmental backlash or Body Point depletion. The Stillness of Dunmar fully nullifies Siddha work.

The Twelve Temples — Domain by Sign

♈ Mesha — The Forge of the First SparkAries March
Volcanic forge-temple within a caldera. Siddha channel their will into crafting divine weapons (Astra). Domain spells: fire, initiative, primal charge, weapon enhancement. The Fire Temple — Lord Aries has ongoing friction with its leadership over military doctrine.
♉ Vrishabha — The Mandala of the Earthly FormTaurian Plains
An open-air temple grown from the earth itself. Secretive geomancy school. Domain spells: earth-shaping, abundance, plant growth, endurance, wealth divination. Lady Taurus secretly funds this temple's hidden research.
♊ Mithuna — The Resonant ArchiveTwin Republics, Aetheria
Library-temple on twin spires. Domain spells: duality, air, language, suggestion, illusion, knowledge. The Voice of Inspired Speech turns mundane words into powerful suggestions. Lord Gemini's Loom of Fate infiltration threatens its neutrality.
♋ Karkata — The Sunken Heart MandalaSundered Coast, Mare Occultum
A tidal grotto accessible only at low tide. Domain spells: water, protection, ancestor communion, emotional healing, ward.
♌ Simha — The Throne of the Radiant SelfLeonine Empire
A golden temple on Aurelia's highest hill. Domain spells: radiant light, authority, courage, solar fire, divine right. The Mantra of Divine Right will only sound in the presence of a true and just ruler — it is growing quieter as Lord Leo's legitimacy fades.
♍ Kanya — The Sanctum of Flawless ServiceRepublic of Argentum
A monastic hospital-temple. Domain spells: purification, healing, disease removal, anti-blight. The Order of Pure Reason studies it intensely, trying to replicate the Mantra of Purification scientifically.
♎ Tula — The Pavilion of Cosmic BalanceSereignate of Libra, Aetheria
An open-air pavilion of serene gardens. Domain spells: binding pacts, truth detection, cosmic equilibrium, law enforcement, flight. The Voice of the Binding Pact makes any oath sworn here magically unbreakable. The ultimate diplomatic neutral ground.
♏ Vrishchika — The Well of Hidden PowerViridian Principalities, Mare Occultum
A cave temple hidden behind a waterfall. Domain spells: secrets, death rites, transformation, psychic energy (Siddhi), shadow. The Loom of Fate seeks to control its central pool — a font of raw psychic energy.
♐ Dhanus — The Spire of the Higher TruthSagittarian Steppes
An impossibly tall spire piercing the sky. Domain spells: far-sight, cosmic navigation, star-reading, prophecy, truth. Lord Sagittarius received a dark prophecy here — its content is unresolved.
♑ Makara — The Karmic Mountain MonasteryStone Kingdom of Dunmar
A remote monastery carved into Dunmar's highest peak. Domain spells: endurance, ambition, earth mastery, time and patience. Currently placing diplomatic pressure on Lady Capricorn to open Dunmar's borders.
♒ Kumbha — The Resonant Geode of KumbhaAquarian Archipelago, Aetheria
A temple inside a colossal floating geode. Domain spells: collective consciousness, resonance, creation, community weaving, aether-flow. The Seal of Divine Resonance causes the entire geode to hum in harmony with the world's collective consciousness.
♓ Meena — The Temple of the Final DreamPiscean Veil, Mare Occultum
A crumbling temple existing partially in the dream world. Domain spells: prophecy, dream-weaving, the unseen world, cosmic dissolution (Laya). Meditating here risks becoming permanently lost. The most dangerous Siddha sanctum.
The Precursor Secret
The Siddha mantras are degraded scientific equations from the Precursor civilization of Kallos. The Eldren are Kallos survivors — their spiritual practice is unconscious science. The Order of Pure Reason is independently reconstructing the same knowledge. Both paths lead to the same destination: the conditions that destroyed Kallos in the Night of Kallos.

The Comet-Magi and the Twelve Aspects
Structured academic-spiritual magic — practiced in the hidden city of Argent Spire, Antarctos

In Argent Spire, 1,200 Magi treat magic as structured academic science deeply fused with spiritual discipline. Unlike the Siddha's intuitive resonance, Comet-Magi magic is systematic — mapped to celestial mechanics, codified into Circles, and governed by Conclave doctrine. Their power source is the Great Comet's passage.

How it is gained
Study under one of the Three Great Conclaves. Alignment with one of the Twelve Aspects. Initiation into progressively higher Circles of Weaving as rank increases.
Source of power
The celestial movement of the Great Comet. Stellar energy harvested into Resonant Geodes during the midday cycle. The geothermal pressure of sealing the Crystalline Core below.
Associated class
Comet-Mage (Wizard) primary. Zodiac Priest (Cleric) can access Epic Tier Aetheric Channels (10th/11th/12th level) — unique to this tradition.
Structure
Male mages study celestial science. Female Sisters of the Spire guard the physical seals. Ice Sentinels patrol cliffs; Trench Wardens descend into ice vaults daily.

The Three Conclaves — Doctrinal Differences

Ethereal Dawn — Cardinal Signs ♈♋♎♑High Star-Sayer Grandon
Position: Proactive intervention — Argent Spire should actively shape world destiny. Magic style: Dynamic, initiating, forward-projecting spells. Tension: They secretly manipulate world events through agents — a direct violation of other Conclaves' mandates.
Earthly Core — Fixed Signs ♉♌♏♒Vael-Kallos + Grand Prime Forgeman
Position: Pure imprisonment — all magical energy directed downward into the seals. Nothing else matters. Unique: Grand Prime Forgeman is an ancient rogue Gear-Forged who escaped the Jade Empire centuries ago. The most zealous Conclave.
Shifting Horizons — Mutable Signs ♊♍♐♓Arch-Oracle Lyra of the Closed Eye
Position: Imprisonment is temporary — prepare for inevitable confrontation with the void. The seals will eventually fail. Lyra is permanently blindfolded, seeing only probability futures. Most likely to reach out to outside heroes.
What the Geothermal Vents Conceal
The warmth that makes Argent Spire habitable is a byproduct of the sealing pressure on the Crystalline Core of Ophiuchus below. Every warm vent in the city is evidence of entropy being contained. If the seals weaken, the vents cool — a slow, creeping winter the Conclaves dare not explain.

Divine Favor and the Orishas
Ashé — Living cosmic life force inseparable from the Ashé-Touched people of Orun-Ayé

In the Ashélands, magic is not learned or studied. It is grown. Ashé is the living cosmic life force flowing through all matter. The Ashé-Touched bear visible divine traits from their patron Orishas because Ashé flows through their biology. One does not Weave a Working; one becomes a more complete vessel for their Orisha's will.

How it is gained
Ritual, sacrifice, and community service. One becomes a vessel for Orisha power through deepening relationship — not study. The Ashé-Medium can achieve temporary full Orisha possession.
Source of power
Ashé itself — the living cosmic life force. The twelve Orishas are the same divine forces as the twelve Zodiac gods, perceived through a different cultural lens.
Associated class
Ashé-Medium (unique Sideros class, Ω, ♋/♒). Also Zodiac Priest and Fade-Guardian. Babalawos and Iyanifas at the Table of Ifá are non-combat ritual specialists.
Critical vulnerability
The Order of Pure Reason's anti-magic field would effectively kill the Ashé-Touched. Their magic is not a learned skill — it is inseparable from their biology.

The Primal Rites
Druidry and Shamanism — connection to the world's spirit through nature and instinct

Where the Siddha find power through discipline and the Orisha-path through devotion, the Primal traditions find power through attunement — harmonizing with the world's living spirit. Three distinct sub-traditions exist.

The Tuathan Path — Celtic Tree ZodiacTuathan Archipelago
How gained: Spells tied to the Celtic Tree Zodiac. Gained by harmonizing with the current season and the sacred tree of one's birth.

Seasonal mechanic: Most powerful spells available during the birth-tree's season — always chasing alignment with the turning year.

The Oak/Holly King cycle: The great political tension mirrors their magic — the Oak King (summer) and Holly King (winter) alternate, and Tuathan magic ebbs with this cycle.

The Otherworld: Druids can bargain with fae Otherworld entities for spells beyond their natural domain — but at unknown cost. The Otherworldly Gate Faerie Mound opens at twilight.
Fade-Guardian (Druid/Paladin hybrid)Noctilith Moon-Stone — key focus materialKeepers of the Cycle oversee the Elder sanctum
The Uji Path — Omote-Ura BalanceMori-Ōkami (Wolf-Shifters)
How gained: Wolf-Shifters gain primal boons by balancing Omote (human discipline) and Ura (wolf instinct), granted by their Shōgun-Kami spirit patrons.

The Omote-Ura Gauge: A tension meter unique to Uji characters. High Omote grants social and tactical abilities; high Ura grants primal combat power and beast-sense. Full Ura is feral — the character loses conscious control.

Pine Shadow vs Deep Earth tension: The two clans have opposing Omote-Ura philosophies. Pine Shadow prizes disciplined Omote hunting. Deep Earth honors ancestral Ura of pack memory.

The Norse Path — Runic MagicBoreal Expanse
How gained: Each of the twelve Hearths is governed by a specific Elder Futhark rune. Magic is inseparable from geography — entering a Hearth means operating under that rune's laws.

Not a codified tradition: Norse magic is absorbed through the land — a traveler gains runic resonance by surviving and mastering the environment of each Hearth. There are no teachers, only trials.

Key runes: Thrymheimr (Hagalaz — Skadi's Wrath), The Thing-Stead (Tiwaz — justice oaths), The Rainbow Bridgehead (Algiz — protective shielding), The Cave of the Broken Oath (inverted Tiwaz — oaths become curses).

The Forbidden Path: The Ashen Coil
Corruption and the unmaking of one's own soul — power from the Hollow Serpent

Every other path involves alignment with a cosmic force. The Coil path involves consumption by one. The Hollow Serpent's entropy does not empower — it replaces. What looks like power is the systematic dissolution of the practitioner's own soul-resonance, replaced by Void-energy the Serpent can reclaim at will.

How it is gained
Through corruption and the unmaking of one's own soul. The 13 Scions of the Coil grant dark miracles to those who serve universal dissolution. Each gift costs a portion of the Fate Number.
Source of power
Directly from Aethar / the Hollow Serpent's entropic energy. Purple Void-energy from the Abyssal Leviathan. Siderium Quartz (Weep-Stone) can reduce corruption but cannot reverse it.
Associated classes
Coil-Stalker (controlled corruption warrior — Lead-Scale Corruption gauge tracks Void-energy). Scion-Pledged (Warlock) with patron = a Scion or the Hollow Serpent directly.
End state
Full corruption = Void-Husk. The practitioner's Fate Number is dissolved entirely. They retain physical form but are driven entirely by the Coil's will — the undead equivalent.

Fang of the WhisperIntelligence / toxin operations

The Corruption Economy
Siderium Quartz (Weep-Stone) reduces Lead-Scale Corruption — creating a direct material economy between the Coil's power and K'uh-la'an's most volatile resource. The Petal-Weavers of Deep Anthos are the world's only safe processors of Weep-Stone. If the Coil controls the Weep-Stone supply, they control the only in-field corruption cleanser. This is already a strategic priority for the Scion of the Perfect Blight.
