[h2]Lore[/h2]
The Geode-Kin are the smallest biological people in Sideros, and also the ones most responsible for the Jade Empire's infrastructure. They are not a sub-culture of the Gear-Forged — they are the engineers who understood Resonant Geode principles before the Gear-Forged existed, who built the first housings that made constructed sapience possible, and who have been quietly essential to every major technological development in the Empire for eight centuries while receiving a fraction of the credit. [b]The Grand Prime Forgeman considers the Geode-Kin the most underestimated Kindred in the world. He has said this publicly. Twice.[/b]

Geode-Kin are born with a passive sensitivity to Resonant Geode energy — they can feel a Grade-A Geode through a stone wall at twenty feet, and they can read the emotional state of a Gear-Forged's Geode heart the way a physician reads a pulse. This sensitivity is the origin of their cultural role: before calibration instruments existed, a Geode-Kin's hands were the calibration instrument. Many still are.

Outside the Jade Empire, Geode-Kin are found in every city that has sophisticated craft infrastructure — they follow the work. Port Damaris has a significant Geode-Kin community in its engineers' quarter. Elethorn's grid-maintenance teams employ three. Ironhearth's relationship with them is complicated — the Dunmarim respect craft excellence but the Geode-Kin's Aetheric sensitivity sits awkwardly against the Order of Pure Reason's preferences. [b]A Geode-Kin in the Stillness of Dunmar feels the Ore's suppression as a specific physical discomfort, like standing in a room where the pressure is wrong.[/b] They can function. They do not enjoy it.

[h2]Kindred Traits[/h2]
[b]Geode Sense[/b] — [i]Always Active · Passive[/i]
Detect any Resonant Geode, Aetheric Artifact, or active Working within 30 ft — direction and approximate intensity, not specifics. This cannot be suppressed except by the Stillness of Dunmar (full ore-saturation), which causes the Geode-Kin physical discomfort rather than simply switching the sense off. Additionally: when examining a Gear-Forged, know their Geode's current charge level, emotional state, and whether the Geode heart is healthy or degrading — no tools required, no roll required. [b]This is the Kindred's most valuable professional trait and the reason the Jade Empire's Geode-Kin community has never been short of employment.[/b]

[b]Aetheric Tinkering[/b] — [i]Innate · 3/Day[/i]
Cast one of the following Workings without a Channel or focus, using Acuity as the Weaving Attribute: [i]Mending[/i] (repair nonmagical objects or stabilize a Gear-Forged's damaged Geode housing), [i]Prestidigitation[/i], [i]Detect Magic[/i]. Usable three times per Deep Attunement. Additionally: when the Geode-Kin uses artisan's tools, they may add their Acuity modifier twice instead of once — the hands know what they are doing before the mind finishes deciding.

[b]Cunning[/b] — [i]Always Active[/i]
Resonant Fortune on all Acuity Warding Resonances. Additionally: cannot be Soul-Swayed or Dread-Struck by Workings — the Geode-Kin's Aetheric sensitivity produces a passive interference with mind-affecting frequencies. They can still be intimidated or persuaded through ordinary social means. The Aetheric layer simply does not reach them cleanly. [b]Kallos-Sworn Enchanters find Geode-Kin professionally frustrating for this reason.[/b]

[b]Small Stature[/b] — [i]Always Active[/i]
Standard Small Kindred rules apply: can move through the space of any Medium or larger creature; Resonant Discord on Strike Resonances using heavy weapons. Geode-Kin have adapted to this in their engineering practice — their tools are scaled to their hands, and their tools are better than most people's large ones. Additionally: can fit into spaces as small as 1 ft × 1 ft by squeezing — they were designed (culturally, not literally) to get into machinery housings.

[b]Accord-Maker Affinity[/b] — [i]Path Bonus[/i]
Geode-Kin who take the Accord-Maker Path begin with one additional Infusion slot and one additional Blueprint known beyond the standard starting allocation. The Grand Prime Forgeman will see a Geode-Kin Accord-Maker without an appointment — no other Kindred receives this concession. [b]He does not explain why.[/b] Those who have asked report that he looks at them for a long moment before saying he simply finds them easier to teach than most.
