[h2]Lore[/h2]
Humans are not the oldest Kindred, not the most powerful, and not the most cosmically attuned. They are, by every objective measure, the [b]most adaptable[/b] — and in Sideros, adaptability is the closest thing to a divine gift that a mortal can possess without being born to it. Where the Eldren carry the weight of a lost civilization, and the Dunmarim carry centuries of cultural insularity, Humans carry [i]nothing in particular[/i]. The Loom did not write their story before they arrived. That blankness is not poverty — it is potential.

The Solar Chivalry is human. The Order of Pure Reason is human. The Ashen Coil's Triad is human. The Loom of Fate was founded by humans. Humans are simultaneously the world's greatest institution-builders and its most prolific destroyers of institutions. They rise fast and fall hard and — crucially — get up again. Every other Kindred has a single cultural center. Humans have dozens, each one convinced it represents the correct way to be human.

The deepest truth about Humans in Sideros: [b]they alone have no inherited relationship with the Aether.[/b] Every other Kindred carries a cosmic lineage — degraded Precursor science, earth-resonance, Orisha biology, construct-nature, wolf-duality. Humans come to the Aether without preconception. This means they can attune to any Path without friction. It also means they have no natural protection when the Aether turns hostile.

[h2]Kindred Traits[/h2]
[b]The Unwritten — Core Trait[/b] — [i]Passive[/i]
Humans receive [b]one additional Mastered Discipline[/b] at character creation and one additional [b]Mastered Discipline[/b] at every fourth Resonance Depth (4, 8, 12, 16, 20). They also receive one additional [b]Attunement[/b] (Feat) at Resonance Depth 1 — before any other Kindred receives their first. The Loom gave Humans no inherited gifts. So they learn faster than anyone else.

[b]Resonant Versatility[/b] — [i]Passive[/i]
When a Human uses a Brief Attunement, they may change one Mastered Discipline to a different Discipline — temporarily swapping their training to meet the current situation's demands. This lasts until the next Brief or Deep Attunement. [b]No other Kindred can do this.[/b] The Eldren have mantras fixed by bloodline. The Dunmarim have trades fixed by clan. Humans have nothing fixed, and that is the point.

[b]Adaptive Resonance[/b] — [i]Active · 1/Deep Attunement[/i]
When a Human fails an Active Resonance or Warding Resonance, they may immediately reroll with a +2 bonus. Once per Deep Attunement. This represents the human capacity to [i]learn from the moment of failure[/i] — the Aether rebuffs them and they adjust their frequency before it's too late. Other Kindreds have stronger natural frequencies. Humans have better error correction.

[b]Accelerated Fate[/b] — [i]Passive[/i]
Human lifespans are shorter than most Kindreds — roughly 80 years. But the Loom compensates: [b]Humans gain Resonance Depth at 1.5× the normal rate[/b] (in narrative terms — they reach thresholds faster because they burn more brightly). In campaign terms, Concordances running mixed-Kindred groups should give Humans slightly more fictional momentum per session — their choices carry higher stakes because their window is shorter. Mechanically, Humans may take their first Zodiac Attunement at Resonance Depth 3 rather than 4.

[b]Faction Fluency[/b] — [i]Passive[/i]
Humans begin play with Mastery in one additional language and Resonant Fortune on all Active Resonances made to integrate into, navigate, or deceive a human-led faction (Solar Chivalry, Order of Pure Reason, Loom of Fate, Ashen Coil, any merchant guild). Humans built every major institution in Sideros. They understand how institutions work — and how to exploit them — in their bones.

[h2]Playing this Kindred[/h2]
The Human's mechanical identity is front-loaded flexibility — more Attunements earlier, more Disciplines throughout, and the unique ability to swap training on the fly. In play, this means Humans are the Kindred most able to fill gaps in the party's capability. The question isn't "what am I good at?" — it's "what does this situation need that no one else can provide right now?" Humans are the Kindred of the present moment , not of ancient legacy or cosmic designation. They are defined entirely by what they choose to become.
