[h2]Lore[/h2]
The Serpent-Kin are among the oldest continuous civilizations in Sideros — their pyramid-temples predate the Duchy Compact, their oral histories extend into Era I, and their relationship with the Twin Dream-Fish cenotes means they have been receiving prophetic contact from a Legendary Beast longer than any other Kindred maintains. [b]They experience time differently.[/b] Not in the Eldren way — Eldren have long memory but still move through time sequentially. Serpent-Kin think in cycles. A problem that will resolve in forty years is not a long-term problem — it is simply a problem in the next cycle. This makes them extraordinary long-game strategists and genuinely difficult negotiating partners for Kindreds who think in decades.

The Red Clan / White Clan blood-feud that outsiders observe on K'uh-la'an's surface is [b]deliberately maintained as cultural camouflage.[/b] The surface conflict keeps foreign powers focused upward. Deep Anthos City exists below because the Serpent-Kin decided, six hundred years ago, that the only way to preserve their most sacred knowledge was to make the world believe there was nothing worth preserving. The Petal-Weavers who process Weep-Stone, the scriptoriums that hold Kallos-era records, the Moon-Petal Lotus cultivation that produces the world's only Ashen Coil blight-rot cure — all of it is underground because the surface war makes people look at the wrong thing.

[h2]Kindred Traits[/h2]
[b]Scaled Hide — Core Trait[/b] — [i]Passive[/i]
Natural armor from scaled skin. Guard Rating in light or no armor = 13 + Swiftness modifier (replaces standard unarmored calculation). The scales provide resistance to environmental heat, cold, and minor physical abrasion — Resonant Fortune on Warding Resonances against extreme temperature effects. [b]Additionally[/b]: once per Pulse, when a Strike Resonance hits the Serpent-Kin, they may use their Reflex to shed the scales at the point of impact, treating the strike as glancing — the attack deals half damage. The shed scales regrow within an hour. Limit: this Reflex can only trigger against physical strikes, not Workings.

[b]Dream-Fish Lineage[/b] — [i]Passive[/i]
Centuries of proximity to the Twin Dream-Fish cenotes has layered prophetic sensitivity into Serpent-Kin biology. Once per Deep Attunement during their sleep or meditation, the Concordance delivers one true but incomplete fragment of information about a current or impending situation — not a chosen question, but what the Dream-Fish currently consider most important for this character to know. [b]The Serpent-Kin cannot control what they receive.[/b] They also have Resonant Fortune on all Warding Resonances against being Soul-Swayed or deceived through illusion — something in their Aetheric makeup has been inoculated against false visions by generations of genuine ones.

[b]Cenote-Bred[/b] — [i]Passive[/i]
Serpent-Kin are fully amphibious. Swim speed 40 ft, breathe water, no penalties for underwater combat or visibility in clear water. They have Darkvision 60 ft from life in the cenote cavern networks. [b]Weep-Stone handling[/b]: Serpent-Kin have Resonant Fortune on all Active Resonances when working with Siderium Quartz — generations of Petal-Weaver tradition means their biology partially resonates with the crystal's frequency rather than being destabilized by it. A Serpent-Kin who studies under a Petal-Weaver for at least one month gains the ability to refine raw Weep-Stone without the standard DC 18 Arcana roll — they succeed automatically.

[b]The Long Game[/b] — [i]Passive[/i]
Serpent-Kin are immune to being rushed. They cannot be magically compelled to act faster than they intend — abilities that force immediate decisions, shorten casting times, or create artificial urgency have no effect on a Serpent-Kin who chooses to resist them (Warding Resonance DC 18, Attunement, with Resonant Fortune). In social encounters, they may always take a Brief Attunement's worth of time to respond to any demand or negotiation without the other party being able to mechanically force a faster answer. [b]In combat[/b]: once per Pulse, they may delay their Moment to any point in the Pulse Order without losing their Act.

[b]Venom Glands[/b] — [i]Active · Proficiency uses/Deep Attunement[/i]
Serpent-Kin can secrete a mild venom through bite or applied touch (requires contact). Effect: target makes Endurance Warding Resonance (Threshold 12 + Serpent-Kin's Attunement modifier) or becomes Corrupted for 1 hour. The venom is not lethal by itself — it is a slowing agent that Petal-Weavers use in precise doses as a surgical sedative. [b]A Serpent-Kin trained by a Petal-Weaver[/b] can graduate the venom's potency: a light dose causes mild drowsiness (Resonant Discord on Perception), a full dose causes the Corrupted condition, and an overdose (rare, requires intent) causes Resonance-Locked for 1 Pulse.

[h2]Playing this Kindred[/h2]
The Serpent-Kin's core experience is knowing more than you're saying and being patient enough to wait for the right moment to say it. The Dream-Fish Lineage gives the Concordance a direct channel to provide this character with information the rest of the party doesn't have — which creates natural dramatic tension around when and whether to share it. The Long Game immunity to urgency is both a mechanical asset and a roleplay note: Serpent-Kin characters do not panic. They may be afraid, but fear in a Serpent-Kin looks like stillness, not urgency. This makes them either the most calming presence in a crisis or the most infuriating, depending on who's watching.
