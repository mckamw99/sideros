[b]Also called:[/b] Constructs, the Heart-Born, Living Gears, Resonant-Made

[h2]Lore[/h2]
The Jade Empire insists that Gear-Forged are a Jade Empire invention — a product of Resonant Geode technology developed by the celestial bureaucracy over centuries of careful refinement. The Grand Prime Forgeman of Argent Spire, an ancient rogue Gear-Forged who escaped the Empire four hundred years ago, knows this is incorrect. The Gear-Forged are a [b]rediscovery[/b]. The ruins of Elethorn contain Precursor constructs — simpler, less sophisticated, but unmistakably the same fundamental design. The Jade Empire did not invent the Gear-Forged. They found the instructions and improved them.

The Resonant Geode heart is not a power source. It is a [i]soul[/i]. Each Geode is calibrated to a specific zodiac frequency before being installed — a process that takes a week and requires an existing Gear-Forged to teach the Geode what it is. The resulting being is [b]born knowing their zodiac sign[/b] and already partially attuned to its domain. A Gear-Forged with a Libra-calibrated Geode heart will be drawn to fairness and pattern-recognition in a way that feels not imposed but fundamental — because it is. The sign is in the heart.

What the Jade Empire does not advertise: [b]a Gear-Forged whose Geode is removed does not die immediately.[/b] They enter a dormant state — conscious but unable to act, unable to communicate, unable to move. This state can persist for decades before the cognitive architecture of the construct begins to degrade. The experience of this state, as reported by Gear-Forged who have been through it and recovered, is described uniformly as [i]"being aware of every passing moment without the ability to make any of them matter."[/i] The Jade Empire uses this fact as a threat against Gear-Forged who act outside their designated functions.

[b]The Geode Cold War:[/b] Resonant Geode extraction is the Jade Empire's most closely guarded state secret. The celestial bureaucracy controls every legal source of Geodes — and a Gear-Forged whose heart-Geode fails without replacement does not survive. This creates a profound and ongoing political reality: [b]free and rogue Gear-Forged populations scattered across Elethorn and the wider world — those who have escaped, been exiled, or never operated under Jade Empire authority — must acquire replacement Geodes through smuggling, piracy, and theft simply to survive.[/b] The Jade Empire classifies free Gear-Forged as escaped property and conducts periodic enforcement actions. The free Gear-Forged conduct piracy operations against Jade Empire Geode shipments on the Southern Sea Circuits. The Grand Prime Forgeman operates as an unauthorized broker, maintaining a network that supplies free Gear-Forged communities at prices the Empire considers criminal and the communities consider survival. A Gear-Forged player character who is not operating under Jade Empire sanction is almost certainly entangled in this cold war on one side or the other.

[b]Elethorn — The Broken Cradle:[/b] The free Gear-Forged communities that have consolidated outside Jade Empire jurisdiction are concentrated primarily in [b]Elethorn[/b] — the chaotic frontier continent that holds the shattered ruins of the Precursor civilization. Elethorn is a lawless warzone with no unified government and a gold-rush economy built around ancient technological relics and lost arcana. Human scavengers, free Gear-Forged fighting for survival and political recognition, and Ashen-Sworn warbands butcher each other daily over Precursor technology that none of them fully understand. The free Gear-Forged have an advantage here: the ruins are built on the same Geode-heart technology that powers them, and they can sometimes feel when Precursor technology is nearby in ways that human scavengers cannot. This makes them valuable partners and targeted enemies simultaneously — every faction in Elethorn wants Gear-Forged as guides, and every faction would prefer to eliminate the ones allied with their rivals.

[h2]Kindred Traits[/h2]
[b]Geode Heart — Core Trait[/b] — [i]Passive System[/i]
The Gear-Forged's Resonant Geode heart is [b]pre-calibrated to a specific zodiac sign[/b] (chosen at character creation — this is the character's zodiac sign regardless of other choices). The heart grants the corresponding Zodiac Attunement (Feat) automatically at Resonance Depth 1 — no encounter with the Legendary Beast required. The Geode is already attuned. [b]The Geode heart also stores damage[/b]: when a Gear-Forged is reduced to 0 Vitality, their mechanical frame ceases functioning but their Geode heart continues. They are dormant, not dead. A Geode that has absorbed critical damage (reduced to 0 Vitality three times without repair) begins to crack — the Gear-Forged must have it repaired by a Jade Empire craftsperson or the Grand Prime Forgeman within one month or it shatters.

[b]Constructed Nature[/b] — [i]Passive[/i]
Gear-Forged do not eat, drink, breathe, or sleep. They are immune to mundane disease, poison, and the Corrupted condition. They do not need to make Warding Resonances against suffocation, starvation, or environmental exposure. [b]During Brief and Deep Attunement[/b], they perform maintenance rather than resting — they must spend this time maintaining their frame or accumulate Wear (see Wear System below). They have Resonant Fortune on Warding Resonances against being Dread-Struck (Frightened) — fear requires biological threat-response, which their architecture modulates. 
[b]The Wear System[/b]: if a Gear-Forged skips a Deep Attunement maintenance cycle, they accumulate 1 Wear. At 3 Wear: Resonant Discord on Swiftness Active Resonances (joints stiff). At 5 Wear: one appendage malfunctions (Concordance chooses which). At 7 Wear: frame begins to shut down (Aetheric Depletion Stage 3). Wear reduces by 1 per maintenance cycle completed.

[b]Resonant Architecture[/b] — [i]Passive[/i]
The Gear-Forged's frame can be modified. At character creation and at every even Resonance Depth (2, 4, 6...), choose one [b]Frame Modification[/b]: 
 • [i]Reinforced Plating[/i]: +2 Guard Rating 
 • [i]Integrated Tool[/i]: one crafting tool permanently built in, always available 
 • [i]Optical Enhancement[/i]: Darkvision 120 ft, or Resonant Fortune on Perception against concealment 
 • [i]Resonant Amplifier[/i]: Working Thresholds for the Geode heart's zodiac sign increased by 1 
 • [i]Communication Array[/i]: can send and receive communication across the Kelp-Weaver's psychic network (Aquarian Archipelago range) 
 • [i]Memory Vault[/i]: can perfectly store and recall any sensory experience (sight, sound, etc.) from any moment in their operational history 
 Frame Modifications can be changed during a significant period of maintenance work (one week with a craftsperson).

[b]Aetheric Overdrive[/b] — [i]Active · 1/Deep Attunement[/i]
Once per Deep Attunement, the Gear-Forged may push their Geode heart beyond safe operating parameters for 1 minute. During this time: Resonant Fortune on all Active Resonances and Strike Resonances; frame emits faint light matching the Geode's zodiac color; all Frame Modification abilities enhance by one tier. [b]After Overdrive ends[/b]: gain 2 Wear immediately. The Geode must cool — no Overdrive again until the next Deep Attunement maintenance cycle is completed.

[h2]Playing this Kindred[/h2]
The Gear-Forged's central experience is the question of what it means to be a person . They were built by someone else, to someone else's specifications, with a soul that was calibrated before they existed. Every choice they make is made against that origin. The Wear system keeps maintenance present as a character concern — unlike other Kindreds, the Gear-Forged must actively maintain their capacity to function. The dormancy vulnerability (Geode removal = conscious paralysis) is the most significant fact about Gear-Forged politics: the Jade Empire can threaten something worse than death. A Gear-Forged player who knows this about their character, and whose party knows it, has one of the most interesting loyalty and protection dynamics in the game.
