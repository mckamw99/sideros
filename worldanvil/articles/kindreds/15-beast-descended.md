[b]Aspects:[/b] Medium, Sign-Locked Lineage, All Primal Essences

[h2]Lore[/h2]
The Legendary Beasts are not celibate. The Pantheonic Fall distributed twelve gods into twelve forms, and those forms — vast, ancient, and operating at a frequency no mortal fully comprehends — occasionally produce offspring with mortals who encountered them during the great transition. The Beast-Descended carry that encounter in their blood four thousand years later. [b]They are not half-animal. They are humanoid beings in whom a specific cosmic frequency expressed itself too strongly to be contained by ordinary mortal biology.[/b]

The result is visible. A Nemean-line Beast-Descended has gold in their irises and a warmth to their skin that does not fade in winter. A Stygian-line has skin that reads slightly wrong in peripheral vision — the eye slides off them even when they are standing still. A Star-Metal-line has hair that catches light from angles it shouldn't. These are not costumes. They are the world's record of what happened to a bloodline when the divine became animal and something got through.

Beast-Descended know their line. The lineage is named at birth through the Loom's recording — every Beast-Descended is born with their ancestral sign burning briefly visible on their skin, which fades within hours but is recorded by midwives trained to read it. [b]A Beast-Descended whose Fate Number sign matches their lineage sign is called Aligned — the cosmos and the bloodline agree. A Beast-Descended whose signs diverge is called Split — carrying the Lion's body and the Scorpion's fate, or the Stag's speed and the Crab's purpose.[/b] The world has opinions about both.

[h2]Kindred Traits[/h2]
[b]Beast Frequency[/b] — [i]Always Active[/i]
The Beast-Descended carries their ancestral sign's cosmic frequency as a passive Aetheric field — detectable by Stellar Cartographers, recognized by Accord-Walkers, and felt (as unease or recognition) by creatures whose zodiac sign opposes or aligns with the lineage sign. This is not a Working and cannot be suppressed. [b]The Legendary Beast of the lineage sign will not attack a Beast-Descended unprovoked[/b] — it recognizes its own frequency, however diluted, and will instead observe before responding. Other Legendary Beasts react normally.

[b]Lineage Breath[/b] — [i]Lineage-Dependent · Instinct[/i]
Once per Brief Attunement, exhale a burst of the lineage Beast's elemental nature. All creatures in a 15-ft cone (or 30-ft line for certain lineages — see table) make a Warding Resonance (DC = 8 + Mastery Bonus + Endurance modifier) or take 2d6 damage of the lineage's type, halved on success. Damage increases to 3d6 at Resonance Depth 5, 4d6 at Depth 11, 5d6 at Depth 17.

[b]Lineage Resistance[/b] — [i]Always Active[/i]
Resistance to the lineage's elemental damage type. The frequency that the Beast-Descended exhales is also the frequency that cannot easily harm them — the blood recognizes the force and routes around it.

[b]Ancestral Bearing[/b] — [i]Social[/i]
Resonant Fortune on Persuasion and Intimidation Active Resonances when the other party knows the Beast-Descended's lineage. The cosmic frequency behind the bloodline communicates something that words alone do not. This cuts both ways — creatures who fear or revere the lineage's Beast respond differently to a Beast-Descended than to anyone else in the room. [b]Aligned Beast-Descended[/b] (sign matches lineage) gain this benefit even when their lineage is not known — the alignment reads on their bearing whether or not they announce it.
