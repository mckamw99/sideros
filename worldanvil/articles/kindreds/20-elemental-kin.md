[b]Aspects:[/b] Medium, Zodiac-Aspected, All Primal Essences

[h2]Lore[/h2]
Where the Beast-Descended carry a Legendary Beast's frequency in their blood and the Elemental-Kin carry a zodiac sign's elemental expression in their body — not morphologically like the Beast-Kin, but energetically. An Aries Elemental-Kin runs warm. Warmer than fever. Their skin is never cold, their breath carries faint heat on the coldest day, and when they are angry the temperature in the room rises measurably. [b]They are not on fire. They contain fire. The distinction matters to them and is lost on everyone who has ever stood too close to one in a small room.[/b]

The elemental expression follows the zodiac's physical associations rather than its personality domains. Aries and Leo carry fire; Cancer, Aquarius, and Pisces carry water; Gemini and Libra carry air; Taurus and Capricorn carry earth; Scorpio and Sagittarius carry a hybrid the Comet-Magi classify as Aetheric-elemental (lightning/void energy).

Elemental-Kin exist everywhere the Aetheric grid runs close to the surface — they are most common near Ceque Line nodes, and scholars of the Shifting Horizons Conclave have documented a statistically significant correlation between Elemental-Kin birth rates and grid node activity. [b]The Ceque-Attuned Star-Born and the Elemental-Kin sometimes share a village, and the locals find this understandably alarming.[/b] Two forms of grid-expression in close proximity produces unusual Aetheric readings that the Conclave researchers find professionally exciting and the locals find uncomfortable.

[h2]Kindred Traits[/h2]
[b]Elemental Resistance[/b] — [i]Always Active[/i]
Resistance to the sign's elemental damage type. The element that flows through them is the element that cannot easily harm them — the body routes around what it already contains. Fire Elemental-Kin are comfortable in temperatures that would kill a Human in minutes. Water Elemental-Kin can breathe in any moisture-containing atmosphere. Air Elemental-Kin do not suffocate in thin air. Earth Elemental-Kin are immune to being buried or crushed by nonmagical stone.

[b]Elemental Touch[/b] — [i]Always Active[/i]
Unarmed strikes deal additional elemental damage equal to the Elemental-Kin's Endurance modifier — the body's energy bleeds through contact. This is passive and cannot be fully suppressed (only damped to 1 point of additional damage through deliberate effort). [b]Elemental-Kin learn to control this early or have complicated childhoods.[/b] Those who master the control can modulate it freely — warming a cold person's hands, cooling a fever, providing light in darkness from the fire that is always present. Those who do not master it are a fire hazard in specific emotional states.

[b]Innate Elemental Working[/b] — [i]3/Day[/i]
Cast one sign-appropriate Working three times per Deep Attunement without a Channel, using Endurance as the Weaving Attribute. Workings available by element: 
[b]Fire (Aries, Leo):[/b] [i]Produce Flame[/i] (Ambient), [i]Burning Hands[/i] (1st) 
[b]Water (Cancer, Aquarius, Pisces):[/b] [i]Shape Water[/i] (Ambient), [i]Create/Destroy Water[/i] (1st) 
[b]Air (Gemini, Libra):[/b] [i]Gust[/i] (Ambient), [i]Feather Fall[/i] (1st) 
[b]Earth (Taurus, Capricorn):[/b] [i]Mold Earth[/i] (Ambient), [i]Earth Tremor[/i] (1st) 
[b]Void-Lightning (Scorpio, Sagittarius):[/b] [i]Shocking Grasp[/i] (Ambient), [i]Thunderwave[/i] (1st)

[b]Elemental Movement[/b] — [i]Sign-Dependent · Always Active[/i]
Each element grants a secondary movement mode: 
[b]Fire:[/b] Immune to damage from nonmagical fire; can move through fire without impediment 
[b]Water:[/b] Swim speed 30 ft; breathe water 
[b]Air:[/b] Fall at 60 ft/round (no falling damage); Resonant Fortune on Acrobatics 
[b]Earth:[/b] Burrow speed 20 ft through non-stone earth; tremorsense 10 ft 
[b]Void-Lightning:[/b] When moving at least 20 ft in a straight line, the next melee strike deals +1d6 lightning damage
